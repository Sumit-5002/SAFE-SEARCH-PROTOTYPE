Common Questions

Why is a website blocked?

If a website isn't safe for kids, we block it to protect you. Try visiting our recommended safe sites instead!

How to change settings?

Parents can change settings in the Parent Controls section. This needs a password to keep you safe!

Found something unsafe?

Click the "Report a Problem" button to let us know. We'll check it right away!
